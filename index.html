Our Story

We believe technology should serve humanity's most urgent needs

Sahayak AI was born from a simple observation: help exists, need exists — but the connection between them is broken. We're fixing that.

🎯

Our Mission

To leverage AI and data to eliminate the coordination gap in social welfare — ensuring every call for help is heard, routed, and answered efficiently.

👁️

Our Vision

A future where no community request goes unanswered, where volunteers and resources flow intelligently to where they're needed most.

💎

Our Values

Transparency, speed, dignity, and impact. We build with privacy-first principles, ensuring sensitive data is never exposed.
